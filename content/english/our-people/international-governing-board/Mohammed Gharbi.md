Mohammed Jouade Gharbi, a former staff member of the African Development Bank (AfDB), has extensive experience in development activities and has built a broad network among donor and development organizations. He earned his diploma in economics from the University Paris IX Dauphine in France. Currently, he works as a consultant specializing in finance, foresight, strategic planning, and capacity-building for both private and public sectors, as well as civil society. Since August 2012, he has served as a member of the Board of Directors of the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS).
